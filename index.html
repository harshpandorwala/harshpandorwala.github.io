The story
behind all of this.

I have been traveling the world for years now. Living out of a bag, building a business on the road, and experiencing wellness in places most people never get to. Ice baths, breathwork in Bali, meditation at sunrise, conversations with people doing remarkable things.

What I noticed every single time was this. Wellness is powerful on its own. But when you do it with the right people, something completely different happens. The moments that genuinely shifted something in me were never the solo ones. They were the ones in a room, or a circle, with people operating at a similar level. Building, creating, going after something. And choosing to take care of themselves while doing it.

I have built a community of 700K alongside my partner Prachi. I have learned what it actually takes to sustain energy, clarity, and presence while building from scratch. The daily practices, the breathwork, the rituals. They are not separate from the work. They are the reason the work is possible.

Now I am curating that for others. Bringing together driven people and world class wellness practitioners in intimate, intentional circles. Because the way you live is the way you lead.
